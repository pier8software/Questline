# Product Mission

## Problem

Boredom — people need entertainment and engaging experiences to fill their leisure time.

## Target Users

- **Retro/nostalgia fans** — Players who enjoy classic text-based adventure games and appreciate the genre's roots
- **RPG enthusiasts** — Players who love deep storytelling and immersive role-playing experiences

## Solution

Questline revives the classic text RPG adventure genre with modern polish. It brings back the beloved text-based gameplay that defined early computer gaming while incorporating contemporary design sensibilities and quality-of-life improvements.
