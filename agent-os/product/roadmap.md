# Product Roadmap

## Phase 1: MVP

- **Core gameplay loop** — Basic text input, story progression, and meaningful player choices
- **Save/load system** — Ability to save game progress and resume later

## Phase 2: Post-Launch

- **Character system** — Player stats, inventory, and character progression mechanics
- **Multiple storylines** — Branching narratives and different adventure paths
- **Multiplayer/social features** — Cooperative play or social elements
